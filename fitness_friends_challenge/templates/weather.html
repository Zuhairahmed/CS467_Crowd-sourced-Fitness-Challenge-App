Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)


Question: What is Friends Fitness Challenge (FFC) app?


Answer: FFC's mission is to get users back in control of their fitness goals by making it easy to create crowd sourcing competitions.




Question: How much will this cost me?


Answer: Free at this point of development, but we reserve the right to pursue other avenues to keep the site running in the future.




Question: Does FFC connect to my favorite social media?


Answer: That is a feature we plan on adding to the site soon.




Question: What countries can I use FFC in?


Answer: USA only for now, we hope to rollout the site to other countries in the future.




Question: What can I do to get on a challenge's Wall of Fame?


Answer: Rank first in an individual race, winners are ranked from fastest to slowest for race competitions
